# Privacy Policy

## 1. Introduction
This Privacy Policy describes how personal data is collected, processed, and protected in the Booking System application in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 2. Data Controller
The system provider acts as the data controller and is responsible for the processing of personal data.

## 3. Personal Data Collected
The following personal data may be collected:
- Email address
- Age (to verify eligibility for resource booking)
- Booking and reservation details

## 4. Purpose of Processing
Personal data is processed for the following purposes:
- User registration and authentication
- Resource booking and management
- Compliance with legal obligations

## 5. Legal Basis for Processing
Personal data is processed based on:
- User consent
- Contractual necessity to provide booking services

## 6. Data Retention
Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to provide the service or to meet legal requirements. Users may request deletion of their data.

## 7. Data Subject Rights
Users have the right to:
- Access their personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of personal data
- Object to the processing of their data
- Request data portability

## 8. Data Security
Appropriate technical and organizational measures are implemented to protect personal data against unauthorized access or disclosure.

## 9. Data Sharing
Personal data is not shared with third parties.

## 10. Contact Information
Requests related to personal data and privacy may be directed to the system administrator.
