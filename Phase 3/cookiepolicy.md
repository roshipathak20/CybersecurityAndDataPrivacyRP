# Cookie Policy

## 1. What Are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device to improve website functionality.

## 2. Types of Cookies Used
- Essential cookies: Required for login and security
- Session cookies: Maintain user sessions

## 3. Purpose of Cookies
Cookies are used to:
- Maintain authentication
- Ensure secure access
- Improve user experience

## 4. Consent
Non-essential cookies require user consent. Users may manage cookie preferences through browser settings.

## 5. Third-Party Cookies
No third-party tracking or advertising cookies are used.

## 6. Managing Cookies
Users can disable cookies via browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may affect functionality.

## 7. Contact
For cookie-related questions, contact the system administrator.
